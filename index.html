What we learn

Body alignment

Learnign how to align body in a healthier way helps us to keep ourself free from injury and allows our bodies to move optimally.

Gentle Stretching & joint mobility

By engaging in gentle stretching and joint mobility we enhance our general health and increase ability to move freely and comfortably.

Breath awareness

By developing our awarenes of how we breath we learn to release tension and cultivate mindfulness, experiencing a greater sense of calm and relaxation in our daily lives.

Why Practise Tai Chi?

Tai Chi is often called the “Grand Master” of modern principles like core strength and stability — the foundations of health and fitness today.
Whether you’re looking to improve flexibility, enhance balance, or cultivate a deeper mind-body connection, Tai Chi is a gentle yet powerful practice suitable for all ages.
Beginners are always welcome, and ‘returning students’ are also invited to refine and deepen their connection with this art of movement.

About Christie
